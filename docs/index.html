Cadence shows you who needs attention and when. Sync your message history from your Mac, pair your iPhone, and stay in touch with the people who matter.

Download for Mac iPhone via TestFlight

macOS 13+ required. The Mac app is not notarized yet — right-click > Open on first launch.

1

Install on your Mac

Download Cadence.dmg, drag to Applications, and grant Full Disk Access so Cadence can read message metadata. No message content is ever accessed.

2

Scan the QR code

Click "Pair iPhone" on the Mac app. On your iPhone, open Cadence and tap the QR scanner icon. Point your camera at the screen — both devices need the same Wi-Fi.

3

Stay in rhythm

Cadence scores each relationship based on recency and the cadence you set. Low scores surface who needs attention. High scores mean you're keeping up.

🔒

Private by default

Cadence only reads metadata — handles and timestamps. Message content is never accessed, stored, or uploaded. The pairing flow is local-first over Wi-Fi.

📊

Smart scoring

Each person gets a "keeping up" score from 0–100. Without a cadence, it tracks recency. With one, it reflects whether you're on time, early, or overdue.

🔔

Gentle reminders

Set a cadence (weekly, monthly, quarterly, or custom) and opt into reminders. Cadence nudges you at the right time based on your last conversation.

📝

Private notes

Jot down context for each person — recent topics, names to remember, or threads to pick back up. Notes stay on your device.

Setup Guide

Get running in under two minutes.

Mac

  1. Download and open Cadence.dmg
  2. Drag Cadence to Applications
  3. Open the app — right-click > Open if macOS blocks it
  4. Grant Full Disk Access when prompted (System Settings > Privacy > Full Disk Access)
  5. Click Sync Now to read your message metadata
  6. Click Pair iPhone to show the QR code

iPhone

  1. Install Cadence via TestFlight
  2. Open the app and allow Contacts access
  3. Tap the QR scanner icon (top-right corner)
  4. Point your camera at the QR code on the Mac
  5. Wait a few seconds — your contacts will import automatically
  6. Set cadences for the people you want to keep up with
